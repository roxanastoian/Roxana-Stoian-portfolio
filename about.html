Meet Roxana!

Hi! My name is Roxana Stoian and I am a Front End Developer who enjoys writing clean, elegant and efficient code. With a background in Fine Arts, I am extremely passionate about designing interactive digital environments filled with a generous splash of creative juice.

I specialise in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap and building Web user interfaces with focus on responsive design, accessibility, performance and cutting edge tech.

Originally from Craiova, Romania I have moved to United Kingdom to continue my studies where I am still currently based. In my spare time, I enjoy listening to music(and pretending I actually have a good voice for singing), cooking and baking, reading everything I can get my hand on (sometimes even writing my own stories). Additionally, makeup is my second big passion alongside tech, transforming myself into different 'people' everyday. An empty web page and a bare face are my perfect canvases!

Whether you're a business owner looking to get started on a app or web development project with a freelance web developer, a developer looking to collaborate on something cool, or just wanting to say hi, shoot me a message and let's work together.
